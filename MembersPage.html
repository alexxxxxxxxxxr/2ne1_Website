2NE1

"New Evolution of the 21st Century"

Group Formation

  • Debuted on May 17, 2009 under YG Entertainment with the single "Fire."
  • The group disbanded in November 2016.
  • Reunited in July 2024 as a complete four-member group.

Major Achievements

  • First K-pop girl group to perform at Coachella (2022)
  • Multiple chart-topping hits across Asia
  • Pioneered the "girl crush" concept in K-pop
  • MTV Video Music Award nominees
